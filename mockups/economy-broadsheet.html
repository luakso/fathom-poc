The headlines say agents moved $448 million. The agents moved $7.4 million.

Fathom classifies every payment it observes — agentic (settled by a known x402 facilitator), contested (same rail, unknown gas payer), or contamination (confirmed non-x402 treasury & batch traffic) — before counting a single dollar. Most dashboards skip that step. It is the entire difference.

MEAN ≠ MONEY
The mean is what the whales make it. The median is what the agents do.
25,000×
The median contamination transfer ($25.00) is twenty-five thousand times the median agent payment ($0.001).
MENU v. MARKET
$0.0001 across just 154 payees is a hard-coded menu. $0.01 across 24,958 payees starts to look like a market. The payee column is the tell.
